High School Students Performance Analysis

Chapter 2 Data sources

The dataset used in this project was obtained from UCI Machine Learning Repository(http://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Student+Performance). It was originally generated by Paulo Cortez and then donated to the University of California Irvine database.

2.1 Data Merging

This dataset approch student achievement in secondary education of two Portuguese schools. Two datasets are provided regarding the performance in two distinct subjects: Mathematics (mat) and Portuguese language (por). After merging these two datasets, there are 382 observations and 53 features. It includes student grades, demographic, social and school related features.
